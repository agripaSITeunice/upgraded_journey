About Us

Welcome to our art gallery where we enccourage people to explore the vibrant street art scene of Baguio City! This collection showcases some of the most iconic murals and public artworks that adorn the city's walls, reflecting its rich culture, history, and community spirit. Each piece tells a unique story, inviting viewers to immerse themselves in the creativity and passion of local artists.



Whether you're a seasoned art enthusiast or a curious traveler, we hope this gallery inspires you to go and discover more about Baguio's artistic landscape. Enjoy your visual journey!



If there’s one thing that will pull you to Baguio, it’s the way art blooms in every corner—on walls, stairways, hidden alleys, and even on the busiest roads. These murals aren’t just decorations; they’re invitations. They whisper “go wander a bit more… there’s beauty waiting for you.”
